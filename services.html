Indian ecommerce market is expected to reach $64 billion by 2021, growing at a five-year CAGR of 31.2 percent. And we believe that there is a huge potential for almost every business in India to Go online & Grow online. Yellow Magnets is an ecommerce business consulting company that is launched with the intent of helping businesses succeed in selling their products and services online.

There are 2 types of businesses that we cater to:

1. Traditional business that is completely offline and wants to go online
2. Business that is already online but find managing ecommerce operations difficult with high returns and low ROI.

We offer the following services:

- Set up online storefront across various marketplaces both domestic and international ecommerce marketplaces.

- We help build and operate brand own ecommerce store.

- Digital marketing of products and services, SEM, SEO, Social channels etc.

- End to end ecommerce operations and management.

- Inventory management and better utilization of stocks.

- Analytics and sales insights for better planning and production.

- We help them in their day-to-day operations: cataloging, merchandising, order management, fulfilment, logistics, returns management, customer engagement, payment reconciliations, etc

- We help in ideating & launching new products, giving pricing recommendations through competition analysis.

In a nutshell, we help the businesses manage everything that is required to help them grow their business!

What drives us is our passion to constantly challenge the status quo, learn and improve ourselves and help our clients better. We have a team of extremely results driven, experienced people, from relevant backgrounds, to help our clients do what they really want to do - sell!
